Garbanzo Bean Salami Salad

Description

A salad I make constantly which is lifted, sadly, from TikTok virality. Salami forward. Beany.

Ingredients

Salad

  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • 1/2 head iceberg lettuce
  • 1 packet of salami
  • 2 large tomatoes
  • 1/2 large cucumber
  • parsley
  • 1 can garbanzo beans
  • parmesan cheese

Dressing

  • olive oil
  • 1 lemon
  • red wine vinegar
  • cherry tomatoes
  • tahini
  • salt
  • pepper

Instructions

  1. Slice romaine lettuce and iceberg into small pieces and put them in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Chop tomatoes and cucumber into small pieces and combine into bowl.
  3. Slide 1 packet of salami into thin strips. Separate as best you can and put em in the bowl.
  4. Strain a can of garbanzo beans, shake em dry, and put em in the bowl.
  5. Finely dice parsley and, you guessed it, put it in the bowl.
  6. Finely grate parmesan into the bowl. The salad tastes best if you use a lot! This is the most labor intensive part of the process.
  7. Combine salad dressing ingredients in bowl. Use common sense on ratios or look up how to make a homemade tahini dressing. I like using an immersion blender for the cherry tomatoes..

Now, ideally you've made a very large salad that will serve you for many days. I apply salad dressing liberally and on a per-serving basis so the lettuce stays as crisp as possible. Enjoy!
