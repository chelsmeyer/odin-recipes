Ben's Favorite Pasta

A mustardy creamy pasta sauce I have committed to memory because it is Ben's favorite. Sadly, no photo exists because it is such a staple. It would be like taking a picture of a bag of flour.

Ingredients

  • pasta (dealer's choice)
  • butter
  • olive oil
  • shallots
  • garlic
  • hot italian sausage
  • dijon mustard
  • white wine
  • lemon
  • herbs (dealer's choice)
  • marinara sauce or several heirloom tomatoes
  • creme fraiche
  • basil

Steps

  1. Heat up a pan and put in butter and olive oil
  2. Sautee shallots and garlic, then turn up the heat.
  3. Separate hot italian sausage into chunks and toss it in the pan. We're aiming for crispy on most sides.
  4. Start your pasta.
  5. Once the sausage is browned, toss in 2 tablespoons of dijon mustard. Mix it up nicely with the sausage and shallots.
  6. Pour in half a cup of white wine and let it simmer. Then squeeze in 1/2 lemon.
  7. Toss in your chopped herbs and either several diced heirloom tomatoes or a few cups of marinara sauce.
  8. Let it cook.
  9. Strain your pasta but retain some pasta water for the sauce.
  10. Turn off the heat, put basil leaves into the sauce and half a cup of creme fraiche.
  11. Stir it all in until leaves have cooked (this will happen fast) and the sauce is orange.
  12. Toss your pasta into the sauce, mix it up and serve.
